The only way to overcome such conditions and rejuvenate ourselves is through "Raam Naam Jaap".It is the only remedy that can bring salvation to one's mind and body. Besides Chaturmas programs, Sant Shri Ramprasad ji is occupied, throughout the year, delivering Ramkatha, Bhagwatkatha, somewhere or other enabling people to witness the magnitude of RAM Naam. He asserts the fact that these Ram katha & Bhagwat katha programmes are vastly attended by ladies. He says our culture and sanskaras are safe in the hands of our ladies. Not only safe but its touching new heights in the hands of ladies. Even when Ram Nam fosters in the heart of even one lady in a family she inspires the whole of the family, nurturing the sanskaras. In witness he asserts that after each katha, ladies, young males and females all pledge to leave social evils and explore happiness within themselves. Hundereds of people are encouraged to quit smoking and even tea. He says that he is just not disappointed even after such environment.

Only the Rose amid the thorns is visible to him. Through his discourses he is engrossed in the ritual of imparting the vision to see the Roses and forget the thorns.
